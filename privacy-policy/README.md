# Privacy Policy for Bangs Rewards

At Bangs Rewards, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, manage, and protect your data in compliance with applicable laws and regulations in the Philippines.

---

## 1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information to provide better services and maintain accurate records for your account. This includes, but is not limited to:

- Name
- Contact information (e.g., phone number, email address)
- Date of birth
- Address
- Service preferences and history

## 2. Use of Personal Information

Your data will be used for the following purposes:

- Maintaining accurate customer records
- Enhancing customer service and salon experience
- Sending updates, promotions, or rewards related to Bangs Rewards
- Conducting internal analytics and improving our offerings

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties, except as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

## 3. Sharing of Personal Information

We may share your personal data with trusted third parties that provide services on our behalf, such as, but not limited to, database management or customer communications. These third parties shall strictly adhere to our privacy policies and practices.

## 4. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained for as long as your Bangs Rewards account remains active and/or until the expiration of the retention limit set by our internal standards, industry practices, laws, and regulations. Once the retention period expires, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

## 5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 6. Policy Updates

Bangs Rewards reserves the right to update or amend this Privacy Policy, at any time, without prior notice. While we will make reasonable efforts to notify you of significant changes, we encourage you to review this Policy periodically to stay informed.
