Points earned through the Bangs Rewards Program are valid for one (1) year from inactivity or non-use of the Membership Number. Points not used within this period will be automatically forfeited.

### Earning Points
Points are earned based on a percentage of eligible transactions, with earning rates determined by your membership level:

| Level | Tier | Earning Rate |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Level 1 | Red | 4% |
| Level 2 | Black | 5% |
| Level 3 | Silver | 7% |
| Level 4 | Gold | 8% |
| Level 5 | Platinum | 10% |
| Level 6 | Titanium | 15% |

### Using Points
Earned points can be redeemed to purchase products or avail of any services offered at any Tony & Jackey Salon, T&J Salon, and Bangs Prime Salon. Points are not equivalent to cash and cannot be converted to cash.

### Restrictions on Points
- Points cannot be transferred to another Membership Number.

Bringing an ID to earn or redeem points is not required.
